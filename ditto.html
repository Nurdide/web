DITTO

A shelter solution for stray cats, living in the campus environment.

Group members:
Naz Ödev
Yağız Uzun In collaboration with Ankara University Faculty of
Veterinary Medicine
& METU ID201 Course 2019
Ditto is a modular shelter which consists of two identical parts, including a large gap and an escape hole.

Parts are made from wood, and coated with liquid plastic for outdoor conditions.

Identical L-shapes include one enterance hole (bigger), and a escape hole (smaller).
Parts are easily combined with nuts and bolts.

Different combinations with easy assembly aim to involve volunteers to the process and to improve their motivation.

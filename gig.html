GIG

A bluetooth speaker designed ready to be produced

METU ID430 Course 2021
Gig is a portable bluetooth speaker which aims to be used in different places; the form and the handle of the speaker enable users to use it in two ways by standing on the floor or hanging. Addition to the hanging, the handle helps users to hold and carry the speaker easily.
Gig is double-sided; it has sound off on both back and front. It aims to increase the effect of sound and to make experience of listening more exclusive.

YOGAMIGO

An app for yoga exercise,
aims to encourage children’s engagement in online courses.

Group members:
Ege Özcan
Merve Ağaoğlu METU ID301 Course 2021
Yogamigo is an app that encourages children to exercise with/without class by bringing friends together.
Provides a joyful yoga experience with various adventures, characters, and stories.
Children can create their own sessions or join a class / a friend’s session.

They can see their friends, photos, recordings on profile page.
System offers 3 different play modes:

“Select Adventure” includes voting to do same poses with same stories by everyone. Characters are chosen individually.

“Just For You!” mode creates special programs by the data collected from the calibration system.

“Lucky Day!” mode creates random featured programs.
